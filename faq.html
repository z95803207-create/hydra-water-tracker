💧 General
💧
What is Hydra Water Tracker?
▼

Hydra is a free, browser-based water tracking app that helps you monitor daily hydration. Log water intake, set personalized goals, get reminders, and view weekly analytics — no account needed.

💡 All your data stays on your device!
💰
Is Hydra Water Tracker free?
▼

Yes, 100% free! No premium plans, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no hidden fees. All features available to everyone.

📱
Does it work on mobile?
▼

Yes! Fully responsive on all devices — phones, tablets, laptops, desktops. Layout automatically adapts to your screen.

💡 Best experience: Chrome or Safari on mobile.
🌐
Which browsers are supported?
▼
  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari (iOS & macOS)
  • Samsung Internet

Internet Explorer is not supported.

🔄
Does intake reset daily automatically?
▼

Yes! Hydra automatically resets your daily intake at midnight. Previous day's data is saved to history before resetting.

🎯 Goals & Tracking
🎯
How is my daily goal calculated?
▼

Using your weight, height, and gender:

📐 Goal = (Weight × 30) + (Height × 0.1) ml
Male: ×1.05 · Female: ×0.90

Example: 70kg, 170cm male ≈ 2,185 ml/day

✏️
Can I set a custom goal manually?
▼

Yes! Click the ⚙️ Goal button on the main tracker. Or go to Profile, adjust sliders, and click "Apply This Goal".

➕
How do I log water intake?
▼
  • Click "+250 ml" for default serving
  • Click "+100" for a small glass
  • Click "+500" for a large bottle
  • Click "−100" to undo
💡 Each tap plays a "glug" sound to confirm!
🎉
What happens when I reach my goal?
▼
  • 🎊 Colorful confetti explosion
  • 🎵 Celebratory cheer sound
  • 🔔 Browser notification (if enabled)
  • ✅ Toast message "Daily goal achieved!"
📊
How does the weekly chart work?
▼
  • 7 circular rings — one per day, showing % of goal
  • Line chart — intake trends over the week
  • First/Last sip time today
  • Weekly total in liters
⏰ Reminders & Notifications
🔔
How do I enable notifications?
▼

Go to the Reminders section → click "🔔 Enable Notifications" → click Allow in browser popup.

⚠️ If blocked: Browser Settings → Site Settings → Notifications → Allow this site
➕
How do I add a reminder?
▼

Reminders section → click "+ Add Reminder". Set the time, add a custom label, select days → click Save ✓.

😴
What is Night Mode / DND?
▼

Do Not Disturb blocks all reminders during quiet hours (default: 10 PM – 7 AM). Enable in the Night section and set your preferred hours.

📵
Will reminders work when tab is closed?
▼

Reminders require the page to be open (can be in background tab). Fully closed tab = no reminders.

💡 Pin the tab in your browser for all-day reminders!
💾 Data & Privacy
🔒
Is my data private and secure?
▼

100% private. All data stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — never leaves your device. We have no servers.

🔒 Your data = your browser. Simple.
🗑️
How do I delete all my data?
▼
  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Clear Data
  • Safari: Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
📤
Can I export my data?
▼

Yes! Go to Tips section at the bottom:

  • ⬇ CSV Report — Full history spreadsheet
  • 🖼 Save PNG — Visual snapshot
  • 📤 Share — Share via apps or clipboard
🔄
Will my data sync across devices?
▼

Currently data is per browser per device — no sync. Use CSV export to manually transfer data between devices.

⚙️ Technical
⚡
Does Hydra require internet?
▼

Only for initial page load (Google Fonts). Once loaded, Hydra works fully offline. All features work without internet.

🔊
Why is there no sound when I add water?
▼

Browsers require a user interaction before playing audio. Check:

  • Device not on silent/mute
  • Tab not muted (right-click tab → Unmute)
  • Volume turned up
💾
Data disappeared after clearing browser cache?
▼

Expected behavior — localStorage is cleared with browser cache. To prevent loss: export CSV regularly, and when clearing cache, uncheck "Cookies and Site Data".

🐛
