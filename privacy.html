📊
Section 1
Information We Collect

Hydra is a client-side only application. All data processing happens in your browser. We store the following locally on your device only:

  • Daily Water Intake — Amount of water you log each day (ml)
  • Hydration Goal — Your personal daily water goal
  • Sip History — Timestamps of when you logged water today
  • 7-Day History — Past week's intake for analytics
  • Profile Info — Gender, weight, height (used only for goal calculation)
  • Reminder Settings — Times and days for reminders
  • DND Preferences — Do Not Disturb time settings
What we do NOT collect: Name, email, phone, IP address, location, device ID, or any personally identifiable information.
⚙️
Section 2
How We Use Your Data

All data is used solely to provide app functionality within your browser:

  • Display your current daily water intake and progress ring
  • Calculate remaining water goal for the day
  • Show weekly analytics and charts
  • Calculate personalized hydration goals from your profile
  • Fire reminder notifications at your chosen times
  • Persist your data between browser sessions

Your data is never used for advertising, analytics reporting, or any commercial purpose.

💾
Section 3
Data Storage & Security

Hydra uses your browser's localStorage API. This means:

  • Data stored only on your device, never transmitted
  • Stored under keys: hydra_d, hydra_h, hydra_p, hydra_r
  • Only last 30 days of history is kept
  • Clearing browser data will erase all Hydra data
To delete all data: Browser Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data → Cookies and Site Data
🔔
Section 4
Browser Notifications
  • Notifications are entirely optional
  • Permission only requested when you click "Enable Notifications"
  • Generated locally by your browser — no external push service
  • You can revoke permission any time from browser settings
  • No notification data is sent to any server
🌐
Section 5
Third Party Services
  • Google Fonts — Loads fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log this per their privacy policy. No personal app data is shared.

We use no analytics, no advertising, no tracking scripts of any kind.

⚖️
Section 6
Your Rights (GDPR)
  • Access — DevTools → Application → localStorage to see your data
  • Delete — Clear browser localStorage to remove all data
  • Export — Use "Download CSV Report" in the app
  • Rectify — Edit profile and goals directly in the app
  • Withdraw — Revoke notification permission in browser settings
👶
Section 7
Children's Privacy

Since we collect no personal information on any servers, there are no special concerns under COPPA or similar regulations. The app stores only anonymous health tracking data locally on the user's device.
